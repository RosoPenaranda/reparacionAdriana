Oil and Gas

Building on more than 5 years of trading experience, Centauro Supply & Trading companies have a dynamic trading infrastructure and diverse market presence.

PRODUCTS INCLUDE:
CRUDE OIL: global crude oil markers (WTI, Brent, Dubai) and many grades of oil priced off these markers.

REFINED PRODUCTS & DERIVATIVES: light and middle distillates (naphtha, jet, heating oil and various grades of diesel fuel); unleaded gasolines and components; fuel oil; and intermediate feedstocks such as vacuum gasoil and straight-run fuel oil.

GAS, POWER & RENEWABLES: LNG, natural gas, power, coal, renewables and emissions markets, along with related commodity indices.

GAS & GENERATION BTU’S: Natural gas, LNG, power, renewables and emission allowances, and related commodity indices and markets.

METALS: Aluminum, aluminum alloys, copper, zinc, nickel and lead.

FINANCIAL: Currencies, interest rates and inflation.

FREIGHT: Time charters, voyage charters, COAs and financial derivatives in the wet and dry shipping markets.
